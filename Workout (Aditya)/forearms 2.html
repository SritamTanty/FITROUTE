Calves Exercises

Kettlebell Wrist Extension

  • Stand up straight with a kettlebell in both hands in front of your pelvis, with your forearms facing inwards.
  • Flex your wrist upwards until wrists are fully extended.
  • Lower to the starting position and repeat.

Cable Wrist Extension

  • Use a handle attachment. The cable should be set all the way to the bottom of the machine.
  • Face away from the cable machine. Stagger your stance so you have a better base of support.
  • Start with your elbow at a 90 degree angle with your palm facing down. Flex and extend at the wrist.

Forearms Stretch Variation One

  • Place your hands together and rotate your arms and hands 180 degrees.
  • Hold at the peak of the stretch.
  • Return to starting position.

Forearms Stretch Variation Two

  • Raise your arm to shoulder height at 90 degrees to your body.
  • Press lightly on your hand and push towards you and rotate the arm across your body.
  • Switch sides and repeat.
