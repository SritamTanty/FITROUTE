Obliques(Lats) Exercises

Hand Side Plank

  • Press your hand into the ground and pick up your hip off the ground.
  • Open your chest and maintain this position for the allotted amount of time.
  • Make sure you don't let your chest fall forward or backward.

Elbow Side Plank

  • Press your elbow into the floor.
  • Pick your hips up off the ground and maintain this position for the allotted amount of time.
  • Make sure you don't let your chest fall forward or backward.

Dumbbell Russian Twist

  • Sit on the floor and flex your knees and hips to a 90 degree angle.
  • Your feet should be hovering off the ground. (If that's too hard start with heels on the floor)
  • Rotate your upper spine to engage your obliques.

Dumbbell Long Lever Russian Twist

  • Start by sitting on the floor with your knees bent and feet flat on the ground. Hold a dumbbell in both hands with your arms extended straight in front of you.
  • Twist your torso to the right, bringing the dumbbell towards the ground, then return to the center. Repeat on the left side, twisting to the left and bringing the dumbbell towards the ground.
  • Continue this twisting motion, alternating sides, for the desired number of repetitions. Keep your core engaged and avoid swinging your arms to generate momentum.
