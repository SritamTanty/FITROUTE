Calves Exercises

Cable Calve Raise

  • Use a straight bar or ez bar attachment with the cable set all the way to the bottom.
  • Walk a few steps back.
  • Pull your heels straight up until you feel a contraction in your calves and then back to original position. .

Kettlebell Single Leg Calf Raise

  • Standing straight with a kettlebell in one hand, lift the same foot as the side without the kettlebell off the floor.
  • Raise your heel upwards while keeping your knees stationary.
  • Pause when your heels are fully extended and then slowly return to the starting position and repeat.

Barbell Calf Jump

  • Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and your knees slightly bent.
  • Shift your weight onto the balls of your feet and raise stay flat footed. Using your calf muscles, mimic a jumping motion but don't leave the floor.
  • Explode onto your tip toes and hold for a second.

Smith Machine Calf Raise

  • Place the bar on your back
  • Start with feet flat on the ground
  • Extend your heels upwards while keeping your knees stationary, and pause at the contracted position. .
