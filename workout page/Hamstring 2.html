Hamstring Exercises

Dumbbell Glute Bridge

  • Lay flat on the ground with both feet planted. Make sure you keep your heels glued to the floor.
  • Place the dumbbell on your hips. Push your hips toward the ceiling until you feel your glutes contract.
  • Then return to the floor.

Kettlebell Staggered Deadlift (Single)

  • Stand with your feet shoulder width apart, shifting one foot behind you. Hold the kettlebell in both hands in front of your thighs.
  • Bend forward at the hips bringing the kettlebell to the floor while you slightly bend your knees and keep your back straight.
  • Return to the upright position and repeat.

Band Good Morning

  • Anchor the band at hip level. Take a few steps away until the band is taut.
  • Push your hips back while keeping your back flat until you feel a stretch in your hamstrings.
  • Repeat.

Cable Hamstring Curl

  • Use an ankle attachment with the cable set all the way to the bottom of the machine.
  • Stand upright or try bending slightly forward.
  • Flex at the knee and pull your ankle straight back.
